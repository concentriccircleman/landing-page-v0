So What is Sentra?

Sentra is your team's superintelligence. 
She is the central node in the team. 
By joining every meeting, talking with everybody regularly, and reading to same tools you use, 
she creates a unified company memory. Using this memory, Sentra makes sure everyone stays on the same page. Sentra can also be assigned jobs.

A VP of Engineering might assign Sentra: "10min before every meeting, send me a brief with context on who/what/why."

An account executive might assign a similar but slightly different job: "... and also include a quick overview of this customer's journey. If it's a new prospect, research relevant facts online."

Jobs can be more complex: "I'm on vacation for 2 weeks. Keep track of all stuff that moves, take my meetings for me, and debrief me once I'm back."


Powered by unified company memory, she has a deep understanding of the nuances, context, behind how any job needs to be done for you.

So, Sentra can do a lot of things. But here's what Sentra doesn't do:

- Sentra does not live on your computer. She does not have access to your device's data and activity. 
- Sentra does not make judgements on its own. Acting on missing information/incorrect assumptions only amplify problems. Sentra will always ask for clarification if there's any uncertainty.
- Sentra is not a replacement for humans. Rather, she is augmenting every person's workflow so they can do better work.
- Sentra does not spy on you. She takes [data privacy very seriously](link to /data-privacy), and ensures you remain in control of your data, keeping you protected.
